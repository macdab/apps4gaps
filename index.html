Apps4Gaps gives you the opportunity to be innovative and creative towards exploring the Open Data freely available from the Central Statistics Office (CSO) Ireland.

Details

Datasets

At least one of the CSO Datasets listed within the App4Gaps data catalogue, has to be used for each entry.

Platforms

To us, an app is any piece of software that can be run on a mobile device (mobile app), within a social platform (social app), or in a browser (Web app) (see examples). We focus on ideas, not your choice of the platform.

Entries

We accept entries of teams of two-three people in one of the two categories: Concept Entry and Working App Entry. The only major requirement is to use Open Data in an innovative way. Check the evaluation criteria.

Submission

All entries have to be registered through the Registration page by January 31, 2013, 17:00 Irish time.
Entry submisions have to be completed through the same page by April 11, 2013, 17:00 Irish time.

About Apps4gaps.ie

Apps4Gaps is an All Ireland competition aimed at encouraging young people to develop ideas and create applications that will provide innovative and fresh ways of exploiting the Open Data freely available from the Census 2011 that could benefit society in such areas as transport, housing, planning, education, communications and health.
